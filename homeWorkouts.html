Introduction

The main point I want to stress for home workouts is the number of reps you should do for each set. With bodyweight exercises, there will be more reps than our normal weight lifting exercises. But by how much? In Jeremy Ethier's video below he states that once you reach 30 to 40 reps per set you need to increase the resistance.
Below are some tips to increase resistance for your at home workouts.

  • Increase the loads of your push ups or squats by wearing a backpack. You probably have some books or even cans of soup to load the backpack!
  • Decrease the rest time between your sets
  • Incorporate pause reps into your exercises
  • If you can get your hands on resistance bands, this will be your best bet in getting the most out of home workouts. Not only do they allow for you to add resistance to movements like the pushup, they also allow you to hit certain accessory muscles that are difficult to hit with only body weight exercises
