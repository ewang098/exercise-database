Introduction

I would typically recommend upper lower to an intermediate lifter or someone who is focusing on strengthening their legs. By dedicating your workout to all of your upper body, you will not do as many exercises per muscle. Intermediate lifters can get away with this because they have established the mind muscle connection. Better mind muscle connection equals better taxing of the muscle. And if you workout 6 times a week, you will hit legs 3 times a week.
Upper lower is also good for people who are in maintenance mode due to busyness in their lives. Simply hit the gym twice a week! You can even weekend warrior it!
