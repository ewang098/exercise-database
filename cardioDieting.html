Cardio

There are two types of cardio you can perform: High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) and Low-Intensity Steady State (LISS). HIIT has gained popularity due to the amount of calories you can burn in a short amount of time. However, many people feel more hungry after doing HIIT, negating the calorific deficit they acquired from HIIT. Regardless of which type you choose, do not do your main cardio at the start of the workout! A 5 minute cardio session is fine to warm up but save all your energy for your lifts!
